About me

Changpeng Zhao, born on September 10, 1977, in Jiangsu Province, China, is a Chinese-Canadian executive, founder, and former CEO of Binance, the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange. He was introduced to Bitcoin in 2013 and founded Binance in 2017.

Zhao studied at McGill University in Canada, where he graduated in Computer Science. After graduation, he worked at several technology companies, including Bloomberg, where he developed trading systems.

Zhao has been featured in various lists of influencers and leaders in the cryptocurrency space, often recognized as one of the most influential figures in the industry.
